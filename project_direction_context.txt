MyMentos — Intergenerational Conversation Facilitator
=====================================================

Theme: Creative Flourishing (Claude Hackathon)

Concept
-------
A webpage that brings different generations of a family together and helps them have meaningful conversations. The technology disappears into the background — it's a facilitator, not a participant. The magic is between the people; the AI just keeps the fire going and preserves what emerges.

Flow
----
1. START — Minimal landing page. One button. No friction.

2. SEED QUESTION — An initial question appears on screen to kick things off.
   Example: "What was the most significant train journey in your life?"

3. LIVE CONVERSATION — Family members talk in person while the app listens
   (mic input / transcription). The screen stays minimal — it's not the focus,
   the people are.

4. AI-PROMPTED MOMENTS — When conversation lulls, the AI surfaces a new
   question. It picks from a curated list of approved questions but
   contextualises them based on what it's heard — threading the conversation
   forward, not resetting it.

5. REFLECTION ("AFTER") — Once the conversation ends, a reflection experience
   surfaces the wisdom shared — key moments, themes, quotes — so the family
   can sit with what was exchanged. Functionality TBD.

Principles
----------
- Minimal UI — the screen should fade into the background
- The AI is a facilitator, never a participant
- Questions should feel warm, human, and generative
- The reflection phase is about preserving and honouring what was shared
